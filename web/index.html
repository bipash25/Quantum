Frequently Asked Questions

How does the AI generate signals?

Our ensemble of LightGBM machine learning models analyzes 34 technical indicators including RSI, MACD, Bollinger Bands, ATR, and more. Models are trained on 180+ days of historical data and updated weekly.

What is the expected win rate?

Our models target a 55-65% win rate with 2:1+ risk/reward ratios. Remember, consistent profitability comes from proper risk management, not just win rate. A 50% win rate with 2:1 R/R is profitable.

When are signals sent?

Signals are generated every 4 hours at candle closes: 00:05, 04:05, 08:05, 12:05, 16:05, and 20:05 UTC. This ensures complete candle data for accurate analysis.

Is this financial advice?

No. Quantum Trading AI provides trading signals for educational and informational purposes only. Always do your own research and never trade more than you can afford to lose.

How do I receive signals?

Simply join our Telegram channel @QuantumTradingAIX or start the bot @QuantumAIXRobot. Signals are delivered instantly with all the information you need: entry, stop loss, take profit levels.

What exchanges are supported?

Our signals are based on Binance data and prices. However, you can use them on any exchange that offers the same trading pairs. Just ensure your exchange has sufficient liquidity.

Can I use the signals for futures trading?

Yes, our signals work for both spot and futures trading. However, be extra cautious with leverage. We recommend starting with low leverage (2-5x) and proper position sizing.

What technical indicators are analyzed?

We analyze 34 indicators including RSI, MACD, Bollinger Bands, ATR, EMA (9/21/50/200), Stochastic, ADX, OBV, CMF, Williams %R, CCI, and proprietary momentum indicators.

How accurate is the AI?

Our models achieve 47-55% precision in backtesting. Combined with favorable risk/reward ratios (typically 2:1 or higher), this creates positive expected value over many trades.

Yes! Our Free tier gives you access to signals for the top 10 cryptocurrencies with a 15-minute delay. Try it before committing to a paid plan.
